About Celar

CELAR: Transforming E-Commerce in Africa


CELAR is a powerful e-commerce platform connecting Nigerian vendors with buyers across Africa and beyond.
With advertising tools, secure transactions, and efficient delivery,
CELAR helps businesses expand, optimize operations, and reach new customers

The platform prioritizes quality, ensuring vendors offer top-tier products that build trust and encourage repeat business.
CELAE also bridges local trade with international suppliers from China, Vietnam, and Europe,
giving businesses access to diverse products at competitive prices.
It integrates 5,000 manufacturers across Africa and Asia, alongside 15,000 vendors.

To support sellers, CELAR boosts brand visibility through SEO, social media marketing, and strategic promotions.
By tackling challenges like market limitations, logistics costs, and pricing transparency,
CELAR drives seamless trade across ECOWAS and Africa, empowering vendors and fueling economic growth.
